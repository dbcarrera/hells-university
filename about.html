Our Founder

Gordon Ramsay founded Hell's University in 1998, in conjunction with his first restaurant, however it was originally named Gordon Ramsay University.

Frustrated with how difficult it was to get a good education in the culinary industry, as well as finding great mentors, Gordon decided to take it into his own hands and create a platform for passionate people to make it big.

Past Alumni


Education within the culinary arts has been neglected for centuries.



Here at Hell's University, we want to open the doors for young and passionate chefs to a world of opportunity and advancement.
Our team at Hell's University, including numerous Michelin star chefs, strive to perfect all students abilities.

All top students of each class will be invited to join Hell's Kitchen, where if they win, they will receive $250,000 and the opportunity to be the head chef at one of Ramsay's restaurants.

We want to challenge the status quo of what it means to educate, and we want to make it our mission to ensure students can live up to their potential and fight for what they believe in.

"Through food, we are united."
