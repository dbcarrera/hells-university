Saucier

The saucier's responsibilities lies within creating the sauces for cuisines all over the world.

These chefs may also be required to prepare all pan fried and sautéed items on occasion.

This role may be the most important when it comes to presentation. This course will train you to be more creative, and a perfectionist who works well under pressure.

The poissonneir is in charge of preparing all fish and seafood related dishes.

A rotisseur is the master of all things meat, from red to white.

You may assume this chef specializes in rotisserie, however in order to excel in this role, one must have a diverse understanding of many cooking methods.
