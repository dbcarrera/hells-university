Library
Our campus contains the largest library in the United States for cooking-related material. Students are provided with terminals where they can search for any books they need, based on the name or even a topic. This library is open 24/7, so any student can come in at any time to read.
The Campus
Established 25 years ago, Hell's University has been at the forefront of kitchen equipment, ensuring all students have access to the best tools available for learning at all times. Our campus also contains the largest cooking-related library in the United States.
Public Kitchen
One of the seven kitchens available to all students during lectures. Students may also book time to refine their skills, either alone or with a group. These kitchens are fitted with state-of-the-art equipment, meant to simulate a real-world experience in a restaurant.
Dorm
All students are given the option of staying on-campus in a dorm, which may be added on top of the course fee. These dorms have a modern interior, with spacious rooms. It also contains a well-equipped kitchen for cooking meals at your own discretion. Dorms are protected by security patrols to ensure the area is safe for students.
